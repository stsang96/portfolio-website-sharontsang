USER RESEARCH

I conducted research on Yummly and Allrecipes, which helped me narrow down the market and how these competitors tend to maintain their branding and UI design. I noted down the strategies and how these brands approached their users, while assessing how they often presented recipes to the people using their apps. Afterwards, I broke down each brand by creating a SWOT analysis, drawing a conclusion that both had different approaches in their strategies. Yummly focused on specific preferences in their customization while AllRecipes leveraged their brand longevity.

USER PERSONAS

The target audience caters towards adults and college students who have time-sensitive schedules. I created these personas based on my interview responses of those who prefer a recipe app that offers clearcut explanations, condensed details, and organized reviews.

USER STORIES & USER FLOW DIAGRAM

Finding out what the needs and wants was essentially important in determining what was necessary to include for my UI layout. It helped me put into perspective of the different functions that could help the user rather than giving them random, fun things to mess around with. My user stories were shaped on what could be useful and beneficial to the user based on what my interviewee particiapants had all collectively agreed on.

MOODBOARD

I crafted two moodboards to determine the “feel” of my recipe app. Conclusively, I leaned towards the board on the right to use fun colors and a warm atmosphere while maintaining a minimalist and professional layout.

SKETCHES

By using the method, “Crazy 8s”, I managed to create a few pages of iterations in an efficient process without being too concerend with making them elaborate and overly-detailed.

WIREFRAMES

I drafted the sketches into digital low-fidelity wireframes, which focused on the home screen, preference screen, and recipe profiles.

RESPONSIVE DESIGN

I began to adapt my mid-fidelity wireframes for the home screen and search results screen into responsive app designs for the tablet and desktop sizes.
